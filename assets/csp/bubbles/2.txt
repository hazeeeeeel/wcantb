Afruza Khanom, "Postmodern Visions: Consumer Culture’s (Re)making of the Gaze," BRAC University Journal 6, no. 1 & 2 (2010): 61–66. Khanom uses the word “ gaze” to refer to “the critical eye of society as formed by consumer culture by which one sees others and is in turn seen by others”. In her view, the powerful language and imagery used in TV advertisements are deliberately crafted to create a false visionary standard which everyone aspire to attain but can hardly catch up with.
